About Us

AS THE CROW DRIVES was motivated by a physiotherapist seeking a solution to optimise his client visits efficiently. Faced with the challenge of managing appointments at multiple locations, Karl envisioned a tool that would streamline his route planning. This vision led to the creation of our webpage â€“ a user-friendly platform designed to calculate the fastest route among up to 5 locations. Whether it's driving, walking, or riding a bike, our webpage provides a tailored solution for individuals who need to navigate multiple stops in their day. The intuitive input form allows users to add locations seamlessly, creating a list of destinations. Once the list is complete, users can generate a response that reveals the most time-saving route, empowering them to plan their day effectively. Our innovative features don't stop there. Users can choose to return to their origin point or conclude their journey at a specific location. As we evolve, we aim to introduce additional functionalities to enhance the user experience. Join us on this journey as we continue to simplify route optimisation, saving you valuable time and making your daily navigation a breeze..
